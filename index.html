My name is Kanyisa Ntombini. I am training to be an Agile Web Developer with skills in HTML/CSS and JavaScript. I am also partially-sighted due to a condition called Keratoconus and as a result, I am a disability activist.

Initially, I wanted to pursue a career in science and technology because I thought it was so cool. However, as I grew up with technology such as screen readers and ZoomText enabled me to access phones, computers and be able to study. As a result, I would like to develop accessible technology as well at an affordable price so that disabled people from low income communities can access it as well.

When I am not coding or doing activism I love hiking, going to the beach and spending time with my friends and family. I am also an avid knitter!
